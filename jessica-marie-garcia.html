Jessica Marie Garcia is the main cast of the series On My Block.

 Posted on June 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  500 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

If you follow the famous Netflix series On My Block, then Jasmine Flores might not be the new name for you. Today, we are writing about an actress Jessica Marie Garcia, who played Jasmine in the teen, comedy-drama series.

The actress Jessica was born on March 23, 1987, in Orlando, Florida, USA. She holds an American nationality. Garcia's mother is of Cuban descent and her father is of Mexican descent. Besides, nothing much is known about her parents and early life.

Romantic Relationship With Adam Celorier

The 33-years-old On My Block actress Jessica is in a marital relationship with Adam Celorier. Jessica Marie's husband Adam who graduated from the New England Technical Institute for Television and Radio Production has worked in the realty series Little Women: LA in its art department.

How and where Jessica and Adam met it is unknown but they are together since 2008. After dating for around eight-years, the love bird got engaged in January 2016. Garcia shared the news of her engagement via Instagram post. 

Married Since 2018

After the happiest engagement, Garcia & Celorier tied the wedding knot on October 27, 2018. The engaged couple shared the wedding vows in front of their family members, friends, and relatives, at The Mission Inn Hotel & Spa, Riverside, California.

The On My Block cast members like Diego Tinoco, Sierra Capri, Jason Genao, Julio Macias, etc also attended the wedding reception. 

On the big day, the groom Adam wears black suit pants, a white shirt, and a black bow tie. On the other side, the bride Jessica was looking beautiful in her sleeveless gown, long embroidered veil by Rachel & Rose Bridal. Additionally, she carried a red rose bouquet.

As of November 2020, the husband-wife duo is living a blissful life and is sharing a perfect bond. Jessica attends public functions with her beloved spouse and she also did not hesitate t share romantic pictures with him on her social accounts. 

Movies and TV shows

The 5-feet-4-inch (162 cm) tall Jessica is a member of the Hollywood Film Industry since 1999. Garcia's debut film is Our Friend, Martin, where she has played a role as Maria Ramirez.

Then in 2010, Jessica starred as Sierra in the drama series Huge. From 2012-2014, she portrayed a leading character as Willow Cruz in the Disney Channel series Liv and Maddie.

Till now Jessica has worked in movies and TV shows like Las Angeles, The Middle, Casual, Goliath, How to Get Away with Murder, American Princess, Dairy of a Future President, etc. 

Enjoys Net Worth Of $1 million

The actress Jessica has a net worth of around $1 million. The primary source of earning for Garcia is from her professional acting career.

As the main cast of the series On My Block, she is earning an impressive sum of wealth. As per The Hollywood Reporter, the On My Block leading cast members earned around $20,000 per episode for the first two seasons and $65,000 per episode for season three.
