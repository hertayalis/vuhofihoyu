Rare Ultimate Warrior Action Figure Listed for Sale at Nearly $1 Million

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  218 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Ultimate Warrior is one of the more divisive figures in WWE history, and he was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2014 before he sadly passed away just a few days later. Interestingly enough, one fan is selling an action figure of The Ultimate Warrior for almost $1 million.

The Ultimate Warrior wrestled for the World Wrestling Federation from 1987 to 1992, with an incredibly brief return in 1996. He was also a very controversial pro wrestler in the industry. After all, he once refused to sign an autograph for kid.

A fan recently took to eBay and is currently selling an action figure of The Ultimate Warrior, which he got as part of a clearance sale. Shockingly enough, he is selling the action figure for $975,000. To add to that, he is also charging $28.05 for shipping because he can.

The Ultimate Warrior achieved a major career milestone in 1990 when he defeated Hulk Hogan in the main event of WrestleMania 6 to become the new WWE Champion. That’s exactly why it is extremely doubtful anyone would actually purchase this action figure.
