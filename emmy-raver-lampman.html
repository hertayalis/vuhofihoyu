    Emmy Raver-Lampman Wikipedia

    Lampman started her career in musical theatre. While she was at college, she auditioned for Children of Eden and won a leading role. Due to this, she played a role for a touring (later Broadway) production of Hair, and she received her Equity card. Also, Emmy appeared on Broadway in Jekyll & Hyde and A Night with Janis Joplin.

    She was in the national touring production of Wicked as a standby for the role of Elphaba in 2014.

    Emmy Raver-Lampman Hamilton

    She was a member of the original ensemble cast of Hamilton on Broadway in 2015. Lampman left the Hamilton ensemble to star as Pearl Krabs in the SpongeBob SquarePants musical in Chicago. She returned to Hamilton in a starring role, playing Angelica Schuyler in the first national tour of the production in 2017.

    Lampman had small roles in a few television productions, such as A Million Little Things. In 2017, she landed her first major television role, portraying Allison Hargreeves in The Umbrella Academy, which was released on Netflix in February 2019.
