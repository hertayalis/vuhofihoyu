In this piece you will get to know all about Blake Shelton children. But then who is Blake Shelton?

Blake Shelton is American country music performer and television personality Blake Tollison Shelton. He released his first single, “Austin,” in 2001.

His self-titled debut album’s lead track, “Austin,” spent five weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart.

Blake Shelton was conceived on June 18, 1976, in Ada, Oklahoma, by used car salesman Richard Shelton and beauty parlor owner Dorothy Shelton.

He was given a Denbo Diamond Prize in his home state at the age of 16. His older brother Richie Shelton, who was 24 at the time, was killed in a car accident on November 13, 1990.

After graduating from high school in 1994, Blake Shelton decided to pursue a full-time career in singing and songwriting. He wrote music, which he then sold to several music publishing houses. Immediately, he agreed to a solo recording contract with “Giant Records.”

“If I Am Honest,” Shelton’s tenth album, was released in May 2016. The album rose to great fame after making its “US Billboard 200” debut at number three. In the first week following release, 153,000 copies were sold.

Does Blake Shelton Have Children?

Has Blake Shelton ever had kids? No, Blake Shelton is not a biological father; nevertheless, he is the stepfather to Gwen Stefani’s children. He spoke to the PEOPLE about the fun of being a stepfather and how he has learned he is more dependable than he ever imagined.
