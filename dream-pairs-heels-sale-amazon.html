When is Black Friday?

Black Friday 2023 falls on November 24, which is the day after Thanksgiving. Over the past few years, many stores, including Amazon, have started launching deals earlier and earlier to extend their Black Friday sales. 

How long does Black Friday last?

Black Friday typically lasts for just 24 hours on the day after Thanksgiving. However, many retailers, including Amazon, extend their holiday deals beyond just that date, offering discounts for weeks leading up to the official Black Friday sale. 

Does Amazon have Black Friday sales?

Amazon hosts an annual Black Friday sale with deals in every category imaginable. The retailer just announced that this year’s Black Friday event will officially begin on November 17, with new deals dropping every day through November 24.

While many of Amazon's discounts are open to all shoppers, select deals will be available for Prime members only. Be sure to log into your Prime account or sign up for a free 30-day trial to take full advantage of Amazon’s Black Friday savings.
